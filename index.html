The Tale of the Tail

Sweet Timmy

Sweet Timmy

As a pup Sweet Timmy lived a joyful carefree life wanting for nothing and playing with his family. He was adopted from the local shelter after his mother was surrendered because her owner was unable to care for a pregnant mastiff.

Sweet Timmy

Now Sweet Timmy spends his days playing in the neighborhood with his family and friends.

Daisy

Daisy

Daisy is a sweet little Irish Setter who came from a local pet shop. She loves to play and swim down at the creek that runs through her owner’s property.

Daisy

Daisy love spending time on the couch watching TV waiting for her owner to come home. Daisy’s owners are looking to find her a playmate. Hopefully they try the local shelter this time.

Rusty

Rusty

Rusty was also adopted from a local shelter as a young dog by a man who was looking for a companion and a running mate to go jogging with him in the mornings.

Rusty

Rusty has grown to be a sweet and loyal dog and spends his time lazing on the porch basking in the sun. PS Rusty and his owner never did go jogging.

Adopt don’t Shop

Your Local Shelter

If you are looking for a pet and have concerns about adopting from a local shelter most shelters have the animals vetted and they are up to date on all their shots. They are also spayed or neutered before being adopted.

Save a Life

Many shelters are overcrowded and struggle to find homes for all of the animal they take in. If you are considering adopting a pet start at your local shelter you may make a huge difference.
